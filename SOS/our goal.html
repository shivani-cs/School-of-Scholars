Vision

School Of Scholars has been a pioneer in introducing world-class education to the city. A decade ago, SOS introduced the city to smart teaching and has been a name to reckon with since. At SOS, our vision is to nurture students who will excel as proud, zealous, fearless and worthy citizens of the nation who through academic, administrative and managerial leadership would implement the school motto

"We Touch the Future!"

Mission

The aim of education at SOS is to mould students into emotionally, mentally, physically and socially responsible citizens of the country who uphold values above all.
